FAQs about Goyang City

Where is Goyang City located?

Goyang City is located in the northwestern part of Gyeonggi Province, South Korea, and is adjacent to the northern side of Seoul.

What are the main attractions in Goyang City?

Goyang City is known for its cultural landmarks, beautiful parks, and recreational areas such as Ilsan Lake Park, the Korean International Exhibition Center (KINTEX), and Haengju Fortress.

How can I get to Goyang City from Seoul?

Goyang City is easily accessible from Seoul via subway, bus, or car. The most direct way is to take the Seoul Metropolitan Subway, with several lines extending into Goyang.
